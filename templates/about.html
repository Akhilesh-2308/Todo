About MyTodo

MyTodo is a simple and efficient task management application designed to help you organize your daily activities. With MyTodo, you can easily add tasks, provide descriptions, and keep track of when they were added. The app also allows you to update and delete tasks, ensuring you always stay on top of your to-do list.

How to Use:

  • Add Your Todo: Enter a title and description, then click Submit.
  • Manage Your Tasks: Update or delete tasks in the list below.
